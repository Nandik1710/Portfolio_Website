Hi I am

Bhuwan Singh

Sketch Artist

Welcome to my space! I’m Bhuwan Singh , and I love expressing myself through art, where I’ve been lucky to win awards at events like IAC, Veergatha 2.0, and ISTF. But my passions don’t stop there—I’ve also earned national recognition in Round Square, received the Constantine Award, and even presented a winning research paper on geosteering. Outside of all this, I enjoy staying active and playing sports whenever I can.

My Interests

My interests are an extension of my problem-solving mindset and drive for continuous improvement. The analytical thinking I use in quizzing and football directly informs my engineering work, while sketching sharpens my attention to detail and creative vision. These activities are where I hone the teamwork, discipline, and focus I apply to all my endeavors.
